Which EV models and chargers are compatible with Easy Vahan?

Easy vahan has the widest breadth of EV and residential charger compatibility among charge management apps available.

Does Easy Vahan really help some EV drivers save over $1,000 a year?

Yes, we really do. While the free app can help you save by charging off-peak, the platform also powers dozens of EV programs with utilities and other partners.

What is smart charging? And will my car be charged when I need it?

Easy Vahan always prioritizes your charging needs first. Set your smart schedule around your lifestyle, and the app will ensure your EV gets the charge you need when you need it.

The algorithm then optimizes your charging for when electricity is cheapest according to your rate. And it also charges your EV when the grid is greenest and renewable energy generation is high.

What if I need to charge right away?

If you need to charge your EV urgently, and want to bypass your smart charging schedule, you can simply prebook the slot anytime within the Dashboard tab.

Are my EV or charger credentials secure?

Easy Vahan does not see or store your EV and charger username and password. Once you enter your username and password, we instantly exchange them with the vehicle or charger manufacturer for a secure token that allows us to be able to continue to access your account to optimize your charging. This token is stored securely and encrypted in cloud platform, hosted by Amazon Web Services.
